Gopher A Bite



Setting the Scene

It's September 2020, the first month of my freshmen year of college. With the whole pandemic thing, involvement in student groups wasn't so easy. Most groups opted for virtual events for the year of 2020-21, including SASE (the Society of Asian Scientist and Engineers). Ever year, a group of engineers in SASE create an annual project, we call this group SASE Labs. Now, typically the project is a mixure of software engineering and mechanical engineering (ex. smart bike helments), but with Covid-19, we had to lean more on the side of software.

The Project

After the Lab Kickoff event, the team of around 20 students chose to make an app geared towards UMN students and food. We decided to call it Gopher A Bite, paying tribute to our much loved mascot, Goldy the Gopher! This app aimed to give personalized resturant recommendations around campus according to your dietary restrictions and past rating while using the app.
